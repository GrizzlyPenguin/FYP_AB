Welcome to the knowledge Base

Below shows the common known error or normal bugs that will happen to customer and we provide a solution to the known bugs or error

>Error 404 not found

  1. Retry the web page by pressing F5, clicking/tapping the refresh/reload button, or trying the URL from the address bar again.
  2. Check for errors in the URL. Often times the 404 Not Found error appears because the URL was typed wrong or the link that was clicked on points to the wrong URL.
  3. Move up one directory level at a time in the URL until you find something.

If above solution is not fixed, please Send Feedback to our service team

>Can't seems to log in into the server

  1. There might be an error while you were entering your email or password. please try again correctly
  2. if you forgot you password, try to press "forgot password" in the login page.

If above solution is not fixed, please Send Feedback to our service team

  2. Press on Register buton on the bottom of the log in field.
  3. Enter your details based on the field that are give to you in the registraton form.
